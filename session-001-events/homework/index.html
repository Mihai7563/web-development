RULES
  • Navigate Carefully: Use the arrow keys on your keyboard (↑ ← ↓ →) to move Harambe up, down, left, or right. Each move counts, so plan your path carefully!
  • Avoid the Pits: The forest floor is dangerous, with hidden pits that can end the game. If Harambe nears a pit, his gorilla sense will tingle, warning him of danger. Falling into one means game over, so tread carefully!
  • Uncover the Path: The fog hides the layout of the forest. As Harambe moves, tiles will be revealed
  • The house is hidden somewhere on the map and is not visible through the thick fog. Your objective is to navigate the map and find this house tile to complete the game.
